About Jacobi

I'm involved with the Combat Robotics Flagship team at UCLA, and I am on the officer board for the UCLA chapter of ASME as the social director, where I manage events and help run the ASME social media pages and website.

I also really love music; I've been playing piano for thirteen years (and counting!) and played competitively for three. Nowadays, I prefer to casually play piano for churches and senior homes.

My Interests

I mostly have experience in robotics, and I’m interested in working with robotics in the medical, educational, and industrial fields. I’m also interested in working with transportation technology, partially since I grew up in Southern California and therefore know how important sustainable transportation technology is becoming in urban communities.

I also have experience in writing and communications, including but not limited to news articles, blog posts, biographies, advertisements, op-eds, technical/laboratory/research writing, and reviews, but I specialize in writing op-eds and policy analysis. I like to look at public policy through quantitative and qualitative lenses, and I have experience in data analysis in policy.
