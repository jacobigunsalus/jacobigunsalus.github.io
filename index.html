Hi, I'm Jacobi.

I am a product engineer based in the Bay Area. I recently graduated from UCLA with a degree in Mechanical Engineering and a minor in Public Affairs.

About Jacobi

I currently work for Ultima Genomics, a genome sequencing startup, as a Product Engineer. Previously I was a part-time engineer for Motiv Space Systems, where I worked on risk reduction for NASA JPL's Mars Sample Return mission, specifically CCRS. Prior to that, I interned at Amazon, where I worked on HVAC preventive maintenance and reliability for fulfillment centers. My first internship was at Coco Robotics, where I worked with food delivery robots.

While at UCLA, I served as president and VP for 3D4E at UCLA (UCLA's only 3D printing club). We won the Community Connection award twice in a row, were nominated for Student Group of the Year, and brought in record amounts of funding during my presidency. I was previously an ASME Combat Robotics Lead and I've also been involved with both Flagship and JV Combat Robotics, where I engineered battlebots for both the 3lb and 15lb weight classes. I also volunteer judged and refereed for the 1lb tournaments.

I also love music; I've played piano for seventeen years (and counting!), played competitively for three, and played as an accompanist for choirs and soloists. I have experience in studio art as well. Feel free to see some of my creative projects by going to my portfolio!

To see more about what I'm working on at the moment and my experience, please scroll down to the "Contact Me" section for my contact information and resume.

My Interests

My main interests within engineering are Product Development and Design, Systems and Reliability/Testing, and Operations.

I also have experience in writing and communications, which is why I chose to study Public Affairs. I enjoy writing about and researching interesting political and social issues, especially those relating to modern technological advances. I like to look at public policy through quantitative and qualitative lenses, and I have experience in data analysis in policy.
